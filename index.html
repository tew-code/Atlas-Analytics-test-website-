What exactly does ATLAS do?

We fill your B2B sales pipeline. We find the right prospects, reach out with personalized multi-channel campaigns, and book qualified meetings for your team.

How does onboarding work?

Week 1: strategy workshop to define your ICP and messaging. Week 2–3: data sourcing and infrastructure setup. Week 3+: campaigns go live. You're involved in approval, not execution.

Is cold email GDPR-compliant?

Yes, when done correctly. B2B cold outreach falls under legitimate interest under GDPR. We use compliant processes, include opt-outs, and never contact consumer addresses.

Can I try before committing?

We offer a free pipeline audit — we'll review your current outbound process and show where ATLAS can improve results. No commitment required.

What types of companies do you work with?

B2B companies with sales teams of 2–50 people, selling $10K+ ACV products or services. Our sweet spot is SaaS, professional services, and tech companies in Europe and the US.
