Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Who we are

ATLAS ANALYTICS ("we", "us", "our") is a B2B pipeline service provider. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal data when you visit our website or use our services.

2. Data we collect

We collect information you provide directly: name, email address, company name, website URL, and any message you submit through our contact form. We also collect standard analytics data: pages visited, time on site, referring source, and device/browser information.

3. How we use your data

We use your data to respond to inquiries, deliver our pipeline audit and services, improve our website, and send relevant communications about our services (with your consent). We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Legal basis (GDPR)

We process personal data under legitimate interest (B2B service inquiries), consent (marketing communications), and contractual necessity (service delivery). You may withdraw consent at any time.

5. Data retention

We retain contact form submissions for 24 months. Analytics data is retained for 14 months. Client data is retained for the duration of the service agreement plus 12 months.

6. Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, data portability, and object to processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@atlasanalytics.com.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for site functionality and analytics cookies (with consent) to understand how visitors use our site. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

8. Third-party services

We use Netlify for hosting, Google Analytics for website analytics, and may use third-party tools for form processing. Each provider has their own privacy policy governing their handling of your data.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, email privacy@atlasanalytics.com.
