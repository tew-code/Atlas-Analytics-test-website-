GDPR Compliance

Last updated: February 2026

Our commitment to data protection

ATLAS ANALYTICS is fully committed to GDPR compliance in all our operations. As a B2B service provider operating across Europe, data protection isn't just a legal requirement — it's core to how we work.

B2B outreach and legitimate interest

Our B2B outreach activities are conducted under the legitimate interest legal basis as permitted by GDPR Article 6(1)(f). We only contact business email addresses of professionals in relevant roles. We never contact consumer or personal email addresses. Every outreach includes a clear opt-out mechanism, and we honor opt-out requests immediately.

Data sources

All contact data we use is sourced from publicly available business information, verified B2B data providers who maintain their own GDPR compliance, and direct submissions through our website. We verify data quality and accuracy on an ongoing basis.

Data processing

We process business contact data (name, title, company, business email) for the purpose of B2B outreach on behalf of our clients. Data processing agreements are in place with all clients and sub-processors. Data is stored on secure, EU-based or GDPR-compliant infrastructure.

Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access your data, request correction or deletion, object to processing, restrict processing, and data portability. We respond to all data subject requests within 30 days.

Data protection officer

For any GDPR-related inquiries, data subject requests, or complaints, contact us at gdpr@atlasanalytics.com.

Opt-out

If you've received an outreach message from an ATLAS campaign and wish to opt out, click the unsubscribe link in the email or email optout@atlasanalytics.com. We process all opt-out requests within 48 hours.
